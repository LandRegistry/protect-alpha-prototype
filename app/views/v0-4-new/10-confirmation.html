What happens next?

Your new contact details have been sent to HM Land Registry (the organisation that looks after property records).

HM Land Registry will:

  • check that you own this property
  • approve your new contact details
  • add your (new) contact details to your title deed
  • send you a tbc to confirm this change

We will send confirmation to your:

  • old contact details (the ones on the title register before you changed them)
  • new contact details (the ones you changed today)

What happens if a fraudster tries to change my contact details?

If HM Land Registry suspects that someone is not the owner of a property, we will open a fraud enquiry. We will not publish any new contact details if we suspect fraud.
