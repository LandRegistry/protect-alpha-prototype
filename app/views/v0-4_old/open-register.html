What is the open register?

The open register is a database of property information in the UK. On GOV.UK, this database is called 'Find property information'.

You can search the open register to find out:

  • who owns the property
  • what they paid for it
  • if there's a mortgage on it

Information you do not have to publish on the open register

You can opt out of publishing your:

  • email
  • phone number

Opt out by ticking 'Hide this information' when you update your contact details.

Information that must be published on the open register

You cannot opt out of publishing your:

  • address (property you own)
  • address (correspondence)
  • lender
  • price sold

HM Land Registry published this information for transparency reasons (we must make data we hold publicly available).
